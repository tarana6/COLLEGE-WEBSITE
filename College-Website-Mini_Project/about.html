About Us » Who are we?? » History.


“BMCEM”, Bhagwan Mahavir Education Foundation was initially established in the year 2002 at Surat in Gujarat with an objective to provide quality based education in the spheres of life. Presently foundation is over all running 17 institutions with the view to achieve this objective the foundation started education at Kundli in a rented building situated in District Sonepat, Haryana in the year 2006. The site of college was selected at the main national highway No.1 connecting Delhi, Haryana and Chandigarh. The selection of the site was also made with an angel to provide education at the door steps of poor backward students of Haryana.

Here it will be relieved to maintain that the college at Kundli was started with an intake of 100 students for B.Ed. course in the year 2006 and accordingly all the 100 students passed out with good percentage. In the year foundation stepped forward for its 2nd step of progress and started D.Ed. as well as M.Ed. courses with an intake of 50 and 25 students. Presently the college is on full swing and have maximum intake of 150, 300 and 100 for D.El.Ed.(D.Ed.), B.Ed. and M.Ed. trainees with a team of well qualified lecturers under the expertise guidance of Dr. Sonia Anand, Principal.


Mission and Vision

We follow the same road chart by understanding the fact that doers are the visionaries first who put their visions in to the act.

“BMCEM MISSION”

  • A passion for academic excellence and effective learning
  • Uncompromising human values
  • A sensitive social conscience
  • An abiding commitment to improve the quality of teacher education

"BMCEM VISION”

  • To enable trainee teachers to realize their full potential
  • To make them understand that they serve as agents of improvement and change in the society.
  • To produce quality teachers with exemplary moral values, who could play important role in nation building.
  • To provide an opportunity of quality education to rural students.
